# Feedback Analyzer

> Turn customer feedback into exactly what to build next.

Feedback Analyzer uses AI to help product managers instantly identify their customers' biggest problems, prioritize what to fix, and generate actionable feature suggestions — without spending hours reading through tickets, reviews, or surveys manually.

---

## The Problem

Product managers spend hours every week manually reading through customer feedback — support tickets, app reviews, survey responses — just to figure out what users are actually complaining about. By the time they've synthesized it all, they still have to write it up, share it with the team, and figure out what to build next.

That's slow. That's inefficient. And most of it shouldn't require a human.

---

## What It Does

Paste or upload customer feedback and Feedback Analyzer instantly returns:

- **Main Problems** — the most common issues your customers are facing
- **Suggested Features** — what to build to fix them
- **Summary** — a clear, concise overview ready to share with your team
- **Priority Ranking** — problems ordered by how frequently customers mention them
- **Duplicate Detection** — filters out repetitive feedback so you're not solving the same thing twice

---
## Vision

### From Feedback → Decision → Execution (in one flow)

Most tools stop at analyzing feedback.
They tell you what users are saying — but leave you to figure out what to do next.

Feedback Analyzer goes further.

It turns raw customer feedback into **clear problems, prioritized decisions, and developer-ready actions** — all in one place.

Instead of:

* reading through hundreds of responses
* summarizing insights manually
* writing specs for engineering

You get a structured output that answers three things instantly:

1. **What are users struggling with?**
2. **What should we build to fix it?**
3. **How do we implement it?**

---

### Closing the Gap Between Users and Code

Feedback Analyzer reduces the distance between a customer complaint and a shipped solution.

With one click, product managers can generate a **structured, developer-ready prompt** — designed for modern AI coding tools like Cursor and Claude Code.

No back-and-forth. No ambiguity.
Just clear context, defined problems, and actionable implementation steps.

---

### The End Goal

A single workflow where:

* feedback is continuously analyzed
* problems are automatically tracked and prioritized
* solutions are instantly generated and refined
* and developers can act immediately

From insight → to decision → to code — without friction.
